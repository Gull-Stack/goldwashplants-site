International Shipping: CIF vs FOB Terms

Understanding shipping terms determines your responsibilities and costs:

CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) Terms:

  • Our responsibility: Equipment delivered to your nearest major port
  • Includes: Crating, container loading, ocean freight, marine insurance
  • Your responsibility: Port charges, customs clearance, inland transport
  • Typical cost: $3,500-$12,000 depending on destination and equipment size

FOB (Free on Board) Terms:

  • Our responsibility: Equipment loaded on truck at our facility
  • Your responsibility: All shipping, insurance, and logistics
  • Lower price: You save shipping costs but handle all logistics
  • More complex: Requires your freight forwarder and shipping knowledge

Container Requirements:

  • M30-M50: Single 20ft or 40ft container
  • M100: Single 40ft high-cube container
  • M200+: Multiple containers or break-bulk shipping
  • Professional crating: Secure packaging for ocean transport

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take to my country?

Typical ocean transit: 2-4 weeks to most destinations. Add 1-2 weeks for customs clearance and inland transport. Remote locations may require additional time for final delivery arrangements.

What import duties and taxes will I pay?

Duties vary by country and equipment classification. Most countries classify mining equipment at 5-15% duty rate. Contact your local customs broker for specific rates and requirements for your location.

Can you help with import permits and licenses?

We provide all export documentation and can assist with technical specifications needed for import permits. However, import permits are typically handled by your local customs broker or freight forwarder.

What if there are problems during shipping?

All shipments are fully insured for replacement value. In case of damage or loss, insurance covers replacement or repair. We assist with all insurance claims and can expedite replacement shipments if needed.

Do you provide installation and training services internationally?

Factory installation services available for large orders. We can arrange technician travel for installation, training, and commissioning. Costs depend on location and duration. We also provide comprehensive video training and remote support.
