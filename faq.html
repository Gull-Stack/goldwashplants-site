Setup & Operation

How long does setup take?

Approximately 2 hours. Level the plant, connect your water source, and start feeding. No special tools or training required. Learn more about how it works.

What do I need to run the wash plant?

A water source (creek, pond, or pump), a way to feed material (excavator or loader), and fuel for the engine. That's it. Our all-in-one design means no additional screens or sluice boxes are needed.

How many people are needed to operate it?

One person can run the entire system. One operates the excavator to feed material, and the wash plant runs itself. Many operations run with 1-2 people.

Performance & Recovery

What is the gold recovery rate?

95%+ recovery rate, including fine gold down to 200 mesh (0.075mm). Our scientifically-designed sluice system captures gold that many competitors miss.

Does it work with fine gold?

Yes. Our sluice design is specifically engineered to capture fine gold. Many of our customers in Alaska and California report excellent fine gold recovery. Read more about fine gold recovery.

Is the equipment mercury-free?

100% mercury-free. Our wash plants use only water and gravity for gold recovery. Environmentally responsible mining that meets all state and federal regulations.
